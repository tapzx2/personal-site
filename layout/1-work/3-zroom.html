Z Room Dance & Filming

In 2015 my wife and I bought a dance studio. Like many studios, it was organized around a central teacher who was the driving force of marketing and teaching. For us it was not sustainable.

We changed the business model.

As dance teachers we had rented space and always found the process difficult. Generally, dancers *really* don't want to fill out insurance forms, have lengthy back and forth conversations about availability, or heaven forbid, pay with a cheque. The Z Room makes it easy to rent a creative space at a low cost.

We are actively searching for a location to open a third studio.
