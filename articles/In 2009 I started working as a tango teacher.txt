# Dance 2008 - 2020

In 2007 I started working at Arthur Murray, Hayward. In 2009 I began working independently as an Argentine Tango teacher. In 2014 Steph (my wife) and I won the USA Tango Championships and we decided to pursue dance full time. In late 2015 we moved to Phoenix, Arizona for economic opportunity.

We opened two studios, taught many private lessons, and worked for Arizona State University, Mesa Community College, and Empower College Prep. Steph was also in the Arizona Opera's production of [Maria de Buenos Aires](https://azopera.org/sites/default/files/styles/gallery_large__1200_wide_/public/photo_gallery/Maria%20de%20Buenos%20Aires_Photography%20Tim%20Trumble_14_0.JPG?itok=AwrXo5_X).

Here’s a [video](https://www.facebook.com/tapia.nicholas/videos/10107126818645137/) of us dancing for fun in April 2019.

After this, Steph’s health started to take a turn for the worse. To continue dancing we detoured. I took up Lindy Hop and Shag and Steph started a coaching position with the [Phoenix Swing Project](https://www.phoenixswingproject.org/).

In 2020, the pandemic detoured the detour and we decided to discontinue our dance careers and focus on new challenges.
